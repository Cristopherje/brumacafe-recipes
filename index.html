Our Coffee Recipes

The Classic Cappuccino

Description

The cappuccino is an Italian classic that is all about texture. It consists of equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and thick milk foam. It is perfect for those who want a strong coffee taste with a creamy, velvety finish.

The Perfect Espresso Shot

Description

Espresso is the foundation of almost every coffee drink. It is a concentrated form of coffee, served in small, strong shots. A perfect shot should have a rich flavor and be topped with a golden-brown foam called "crema".

The Creamy Mocha

Description

A mocha is the perfect marriage between coffee and hot chocolate. It combines the intensity of espresso with sweet chocolate syrup and steamed milk, often topped with whipped cream. It is a dessert-like treat for coffee lovers.
